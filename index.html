A little about yourself

My name is Pavel, from the very early childhood I wanted to learn how to draw, but due to the lack of a good educational environment in my city-I did not succeed. Although it may seem strange, because the question arises, how can you not be able to draw and create works of this level.

After school, I had a question about where I should go. I did not apply for design, because I did not know how to draw, and everyone around me said that you can't earn money from pictures. And as a result, I entered the university on the profile "mathematics-computer science", I very much hoped that there would be a subject related to graphics.

At the institute, I met a man who brought a SLR camera to class. I was curious. Over time, he taught me all the subtleties of working with a camera, and for my birthday, I begged for the same camera as a gift. After that, I worked as a photographer, but over time I realized that I was able to work much better with the photo itse lf than with people, and soon I completely switched to working with the frame. And soon I switched to photobash, over time I began to get better and better, which turned into what I do now.
